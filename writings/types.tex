\section{不确定性的定义}
不同的系统中可能存在的不确定性（uncertainty）大概可以分为以下几种：

\begin{itemize}
    \item[认知不确定性] \textbf{epistemic} 
    Epistemic uncertainty arises from a lack of knowledge about the system or phenomenon of interest. 
    Epistemic uncertainty can also be referred to as information ambiguity, ignorance, reducible uncertainty, or subjective uncertainty. 
    缺少对系统或现象的了解会导致epistemic uncertainty。
    它也可以被称为信息模糊、无知、可减少的不确定性或主观不确定性。
    \item[随机不确定性] \textbf{aleatoric} 
    Aleatoric uncertainty arises due to the random nature in the process. 
    Therefore, it can also be referred to as inherent random or stochastic uncertainty and in some systems it is known as physical variability. 
    过程的随机性会导致aleatoric uncertainty。
    因此，它也可以被称为固有的随机或随机不确定性，在某些系统中被称为物理变异。
    \item[本体不确定性] \textbf{ontological} 
    Using inappropriate methodology or belief systems results in ontological uncertainty. 
    Data, models, or methodologies can be affected by ontological uncertainty which is unrecognized and unquantifiable. 
    使用不适当的方法或信念系统会导致ontological uncertainty。
    数据、模型或方法可能会受到未被识别和无法量化的ontological uncertainty的影响。
\end{itemize}
